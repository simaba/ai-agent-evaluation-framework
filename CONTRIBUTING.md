# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving this repository.

## Scope

This repository focuses on practical evaluation of agent systems. Helpful contributions improve clarity, rigor, and operational usefulness.

## Good contributions

Examples of helpful contributions include:
- improving evaluation definitions,
- strengthening scorecards,
- adding realistic comparison examples,
- clarifying trade-offs,
- correcting inaccuracies.

## Contribution expectations

Please keep contributions:
- concise,
- evidence-oriented,
- implementation-aware,
- free of proprietary or confidential material.
